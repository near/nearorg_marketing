NEAR Ecosystem at ETHDenver

FEBRUARY 23 – MARCH 3, 2024

ETHDenver 2024 is officially upon us, and this year the event promises a dynamic week of hackathons, talks, meet-ups, and parties. The NEAR Ecosystem is serving a multitude of Web3 delicacies, including Chain Abstraction and AI. Want to come hang out with the brightest minds in Web3? Join us at our side events and parties. Space is limited so be sure to get on the lists ASAP.

Oh, and in case you haven’t heard, you might want to follow the black dragon and sign up for the Chain Abstraction tea party where the real tea will be served.

What to expect:

On-chain rewards, airdrops, and more, powered by NEAR Ecosystem projects.

Engage with a diverse group of Developers, Community Members, and Ecosystem Partners, all unified by an interest in the innovative potential of the Open Web.

Chain Abstraction Day

On February 28th, don’t miss our exclusive Chain Abstraction Day at Improper City, spotlighting the transformative potential of chain abstraction in partnership with Agoric and Frontier Labs. It’s an apply to attend event and space is limited.

Near in Etherland Booth

Once the National Western Complex is open on the 29th, stop by the NEAR in ETHERLAND Ecosystem Booth #314 and go down the rabbit holes of Chain Abstraction, Data Availability, and much more. The tea party experience will be offered at 12, 2, and 4 each day. Don’t miss it.
